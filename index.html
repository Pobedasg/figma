News of cultural life

The album "Zembodzhu" by Kurgan & Agregat


The Kurgan & Agregat band pleases its
fans with a new musical masterpiece - the album "Zembodzhu". This album is a sophisticated  combination of modern and retro music that takes
listeners back to the unforgettable 80s. It is
distinguished by its special atmosphere, lyrics and
deep humor, which have always been characteristic
of the band.


The album "Zembozhu" is a journey through time, where classical genres
and modern rhythms merge into a single musical image.

The movie “Luxemburg, Luxemburg”

 The members of the Kurgan & Agregat band, Amil and Ramil Nasirov, not only impress with their music, but also demonstrate their acting talents. Director Antonio Lukic presented the movie "Luxembourg", in which they played the main roles. This comedy with witty humor tells the story of twin brothers who go in search of their father. The movie impressed the audience with its storyline and the acting of the musicians, who demonstrated their talent not only on the music stage but also on the movie screens.

Charity
Together with their fans and participants in charity events, Kurgan & Agregat has raised a significant amount of money to support the Armed Forces of Ukraine and the humanitarian needs of our country. This team continues to actively raise funds for the military and regularly donates a portion of the profits from their performances to charity.




The band is defined not only by their musical achievements, but also by their dedication to Ukraine and patriotism, which makes them not only good musicians or actors, but also an example for all Ukrainians.

Biography

The band members (Zhenya Volodchenko, Amil Nasirov, Ramil Nasirov) were born and raised in the small town of Blyzniuky in the Kharkiv region. They talk about their childhood with laughter and enthusiasm: running away from their parents, skipping school and stealing metal. They wrote humorous rap lyrics and filmed funny performances on their smartphones and posted them on YouTube.

One of their videos (the music video for the track "Love") went viral on the Internet, and soon afterwards Daria Astafyeva heard about them, with whom they made a joint track "Teacher". Dasha can also be seen in other videos by this band.
The band's first works can be described as "rustic aesthetics". The tracks of the rap collective are an assortment of songs about rural toilets, socks with flip-flops and cigarettes. The guys have not forgotten about the use of surzhik. Fans say that it is enough to listen to at least one track of the band to "suppress" the melancholy that has come over them.

Over the next few years, the band released single singles and EPs that kept their fans entertained. They also toured Ukraine, not only in big cities but also in the provinces. During this time, the band gained a relatively large fanbase and popularity, and filming in the film added to their recognition and credibility


In 2021, the band released the album Zembodzhu and later went on tour in Ukraine, but due to the Russian invasion, the musicians suspended their activities.
One of the band members, Yevhen Volodchenko, joined the Armed Forces of Ukraine, and the Nasirov brothers actively volunteer and raise funds to support the army.

Now the band has partially resumed its activities, as they perform only when Yevhen is released for rotation. However, at this time, in addition to volunteering, the Nasirov brothers do stand-up performances and attend various podcasts or shows, where they answer all questions frankly and sincerely and each time involve people in donations and fundraising for the Armed Forces.
